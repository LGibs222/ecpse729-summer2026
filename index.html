⚜ — About / The Course

An honest ethics
course for future
BCBAs.

Last updated:
2026 / 05 / 26

ECPSE 729 is the ethics-and-professionalism requirement in the BCBA Subplan at Queens College. Most ethics courses ask students to memorize the Code. This one assumes you can find it on your phone — and asks what you do when nobody is checking.

01

Foundations & the Self

The history that shaped behavior-analytic ethics. The 2022 Code. The 11-step decision model. Cultural responsiveness as foundation, not afterthought.

02

Effective Treatment & Critics

Assent vs. consent. Trauma-informed practice. Listening seriously to autistic self-advocacy and the ABA reckoning.

03

Supervision & the Digital Practitioner

Supervision in fieldwork, RBT, and school contexts. Telehealth. AI-assisted clinical work. The restraint debates that won't go away.

04

Enforcement & the Ethical BA

Section 6.0, the Code-Enforcement Procedures, real Notices of Disciplinary Action, and the capstone integration.

Getting Help · Three doors

If you're stuck.

Async work fails when you wait too long to ask. Three doors, in order of how soon to use each.

01

Content questions

Post in the Brightspace Q&A discussion. I check it daily. Other students benefit when you ask in public — and your question is almost certainly not just yours.

02

Tech & access issues

Brightspace, video playback, Edpuzzle, account access — contact QC's Center for Excellence in Teaching, Learning, and Leadership first; they're faster than I am for platform issues. Email me to flag it once you've opened a ticket.

03

Accommodations

If you have an accommodation letter from QC's Office of Special Services, forward it to me by end of Week 1. Don't wait until Week 4 — most accommodations work best when set up early.

04

Academic integrity & AI

See the syllabus's AI/GenAI policy — short version: brainstorming, outlining, and editing are fine; submitting AI-authored ethical reasoning is not. When in doubt, disclose at the top of your submission. The Code itself (1.01) asks the same of you.

How the course runs

Asynchronous, weekly, honest.

Each week unlocks one module on Sunday and closes the following Sunday. Everything you need lives on this site; everything you submit lives in Brightspace.
